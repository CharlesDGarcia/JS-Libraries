Hello, Traveler! Ready for a Fall Getaway?

Swap chilly weather and city commutes for island breezes and crystal-clear waters. This Fall, answer the call of The Bahamas—where golden sunshine, calm turquoise seas, and balmy 30°C days await. Fly off to four paradise-perfect destinations—Nassau, Freeport, Great Exuma, and San Salvador—and enjoy endless beaches, incredible seafood, and unforgettable island experiences. Plus, with expanded nonstop flights departing weekly from Toronto, Montreal, Calgary, Ottawa, Hamilton, and Halifax, your tropical Fall escape is closer than ever.

    Nassau is where vibrant island culture meets postcard-perfect beaches. From its powder-soft sands and shimmering turquoise waters to its colorful colonial architecture and lively markets, the capital of The Bahamas blends relaxation, adventure, and history with effortless island charm.
